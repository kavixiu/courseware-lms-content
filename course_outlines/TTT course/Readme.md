# Approach
This folder contains the details for the Puppet Train the Trainer session. Our goal for a train the trainer is to enable instructors to successfully teach adults and represent Puppet, and provide course-specific information for new or updated courses to accelerate onboarding and course delivery.

## General Train the Trainer
**Medium:** virtual via LearnDot

**Length:** 15 mins

**Audience:** All Puppet instructors, employee or partner

**Purpose:** Establish a consistent Puppet-style approach to teaching and learning; provide students with high-quality technical training that drives product adoption; provide instructors with support and resources to successfuly lead classes and deal with problems.

## Course Specific Train the Trainer
**Medium:** Live and recorded/virtual via LearnDot

**Length:** 60 mins

**Audience:** All Puppet instructors, employee or partner

**Purpose:** Walk instructors through how to deliver the new course and use new classroom enrvionment; provide instructors with detailed design document so they can plan their teaching strategy; answer questions and provide clarity on approach and content prior to course launch.
