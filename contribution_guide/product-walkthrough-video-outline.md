# Product Walkthrough Video

**Description:** A high level video that takes the viewer through an overview of product features and describes the value of the product and a quick glimpse into how it works. 

**Presentation:** Command-line screencast along with voiceover narration by a subject matter expert. Contains title card intro and Puppet-brand animated outro.

**Length:** Up to 10 minutes.

**Quality/Tone:** Freeform, somewhat casual, but professional.

**A/V Recording Settings:** Recorded “live” using Screenflow or similar software; screen set to 1920 x 1080 pixels.
